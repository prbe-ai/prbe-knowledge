1. Check rollout:

   kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

2. Reach the retrieval API in-cluster (port-forward for a quick test):

   kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "probe-knowledge.fullname" . }}-retrieval {{ .Values.retrieval.port }}:{{ .Values.retrieval.port }}

   Then call it with your KNOWLEDGE_API_TOKEN:

   curl -s localhost:{{ .Values.retrieval.port }}/health
   curl -s -H "Authorization: Bearer $KNOWLEDGE_API_TOKEN" \
     "localhost:{{ .Values.retrieval.port }}/query?q=hello"
